The UCP 600 Obsession

There is a peculiar beauty in the Uniform Customs and Practice for Documentary Credits. Article by article, sub-article by sub-article, it constructs a cathedral of financial trust. I have made it my mission to know every corner of this cathedral, to understand not just the letter of the law but its spirit.

"A presentation including one or more discrepancies is a discrepant presentation." — UCP 600 Article 2

In my world, discrepancies are not merely errors—they are disruptions in the sacred flow of commerce. My track record stands pristine: zero discrepancies in Letter of Credit documentation. Not because I am lucky, but because I am relentless.
